Mona Lisa by Leonardo Da Vinci

The Mona Lisa painting only became famous because it was stolen. During the summer of 1911 the painting was stolen. Newspapers across the globe wrote about this crime. It became even more popular when two years later the painting was recovered.

Portrait of Dr.Gatchet by Leonardo Da Vinci

This is Van Gough's most expensive piece, that was sold at an auction for 82.5 million dollars, but mysteriously disappeared afterwards.

The Girl With the Pearl Earring by Johannes Vermeer

No one knows for sure who the girl in the painting is.Theories on who the girl with the pearl earring was led to a movie being made as well as a novel.
