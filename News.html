Jeff Bezos and Amazon

Jeff Bezos sells 522k of his Amazon shares, which is estimated to be worth $1.3 billion after taxes. Even after selling shares, he still remains the wealthiest man alive with $187.1 billion. He claims to be seeling so much shares at a short period of time because he wants to fund his other projects and bussiness, like the Washington Post and Blue Origin. With this in mind, he will resign as CEO of Amazon and remain chairman in the near future.

Retail Prices Rising

Retail sales have experienced an increase in the month of July. Different factors, like the vaccination against Covid-19, stimulus checks, low interest rates, etc, are responsible for this effect. Many people have used their money to buy items, like televisions, clothing, etc, leading to the increasement and boom in the U.S economy.

Bezos Involvement With NASA

Returning back with the conversation about Bezos, he plans on using his own money to invest into NASA. It has been rumored that he wants to contribute $2 billion dollars in exchange for permission to build a lunar lander to use in the Moon. In addition, Bezos had recently launched into space for about ten minutes. He has showed interest in space and plans to create a tourism that will take individuals and tourists to Space.
