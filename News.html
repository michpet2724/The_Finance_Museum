Steve Jobs and the NFT

Steve Job's 1973 handwritten job application is for bid in an auction. Currently, you have the opportunity to buy the NFT or the original copy. In this application, he discussed his tech and engineering skills, which later on helped him co-found Apple. With the job application being of high demand, we can be seeing it sell for thousands or even millions of dollars.
